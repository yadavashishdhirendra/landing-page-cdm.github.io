Development Branding

We do understand the importance of branding in order to provide a business an extra edge; so we’ve come up with some customized stationeries which level-up their branding game. No more to say, let the stationery do the talking!

When we first started working with Fletcher Homes, they did not have any brand identity for each of their developments across Shropshire.

Following the release of their Shrewsbury site, Berrington Meadows, we evolved development branding we had created years earlier, using the first letters from the development name to create a sub logo. This was enhanced with a light blue colour to define the developments.

In 2022 we evolved the development branding further, introducing a soft meadow inspired colour palette that compliments the Fletcher Homes blue perfectly. Each development logo includes the first letters, as previous, but are enhanced with a soft pop of colour and the implementation of either a flower or leaf, which weaves from the letter. This means each development brand logo is fully unique to the location and USPs of the homes. This icon is still ‘housed’ within a box, which sits under the main Fletcher Homes branding.

Print Design

Print is widely used within the housing development market and sales brochures in particular are a great sales and brand recall tool.

We provide Fletcher Homes with the design, copy creation, and print of all sales brochures for each development, adapting each to meet the unique selling points of the specific development and adapting content and layout as buyer trends and business objectives change. We’ve also provided Fletchers with other printed materials, including publication adverts, sales particulars and materials used by sales partners and within their own Sales & Marketing Suite.
